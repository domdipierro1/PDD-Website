Terms of Service

Practical booking terms for customers of PDD Cleaning Services.

Company identity: PDD Cleaning Services is a trading name of PDD Services Limited, registered in England & Wales. Company number: 17329999.

Quote basis

Quotes are based on the service requested, property size, condition, access, location, preferred date, parking and any add-ons. If the property is materially different from what was described, the quote may need to be revised before work continues.

Scope of work

The agreed quote should confirm the services included. Extra services such as oven cleaning, window cleaning, jet washing or waste clearance are only included where agreed.

Access and parking

The customer is responsible for providing safe and timely access, including keys, codes, concierge details, parking information or agent arrangements where needed. Delays or undisclosed access issues may affect the booking.

Utilities and safe working conditions

Electricity, hot water, lighting and safe access should be available unless agreed otherwise. We may be unable to complete some tasks if required utilities or safe conditions are not available.

Payment

Payment amount, due date and payment method should be confirmed before booking or before completion, depending on the job arrangement.

Cancellation and changes

Please tell us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or change a booking. Late cancellations, failed access or major last-minute changes may result in a fee where reasonable and previously communicated.

48-hour re-clean guarantee

If a covered area within the agreed scope is missed, notify us within 48 hours with photos. Where accepted, we will arrange a re-clean of the specific missed area.

The guarantee does not cover wear and tear, pre-existing damage, permanent staining, mould damage, heavy limescale damage, pests, unsafe areas, or work outside the agreed scope.

Damage reporting

Any damage concern should be reported within 48 hours of job completion with photos and relevant details so it can be reviewed properly.

Contractor fulfilment

Cleaning services may be carried out by vetted cleaning contractors working on behalf of PDD Cleaning Services.

Insurance and liability

Services are arranged with insurance considerations in place. Any claim is reviewed based on the facts, evidence, agreed scope, policy terms and applicable law.

Consumer cancellation rights

Where consumer cancellation rights apply, you may be asked to confirm that you want work to begin before the cancellation period ends.
