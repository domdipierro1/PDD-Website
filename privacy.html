Privacy Policy

How PDD Cleaning Services handles enquiry, booking and job information.

Who we are: PDD Services Limited trading as PDD Cleaning Services. Contact: info@pddcleaningservices.co.uk or 07568 273696.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address if supplied, postcode, property address, service details, preferred date, access notes, parking notes, property condition notes, photos where relevant, and booking or payment details.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, arrange bookings, complete cleaning jobs, manage customer service, handle complaints, keep records and meet legal or accounting obligations.

Who we share it with

Where necessary, information may be shared with assigned cleaners, website/form providers, payment or accounting providers, and professional advisers. We only share what is reasonably needed for the purpose.

Photos

Before and after photos may be used for quality control, job records and complaint handling. Photos should only be used for marketing where suitable permission has been given.

How long we keep information

Enquiry and booking information is kept for as long as reasonably needed for business, customer service, legal and accounting purposes.

Your rights

You can contact us to request access, correction, deletion or restriction of your personal information where applicable.

Updates

This policy may be updated as the website, booking process or business operations develop.
