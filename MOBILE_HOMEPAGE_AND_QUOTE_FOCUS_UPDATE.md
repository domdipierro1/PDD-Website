# Mobile homepage and quote focus update

Desktop styles are left untouched.

Mobile-only changes:
- Homepage hero tile made smaller and cleaner.
- Quote tile appears much sooner on the first mobile screen.
- Once the customer taps/types in the enquiry form, the form enters full-screen quote focus mode.
- In quote focus mode, only the quote form tile is visible.
- Each form step stays locked inside the screen with Back/Next at the bottom.
- Calendar overlay remains inside the full-screen quote tile.
